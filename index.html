Hi! This is an interpretation of Conway's Game of Life

by Ege Ozgirin

You can create your own starting configuration by clicking on the canvas above in Pause or Clean mode. Randomize initiates a random configuration.

Rules of the Game from Wikipedia: Every cell interacts with its eight neighbours, that are the cells that are horizontally, vertically, or diagonally adjacent, according to the following rules:

  • Any live cell with fewer than two live neighbours dies (under-population).
  • Any live cell with two or three live neighbours lives on to the next generation.
  • Any live cell with more than three live neighbours dies (over-population).
  • Any dead cell with exactly three live neighbours becomes a live cell (reproduction).
